Scrivs Privacy Policy

Interpretations and Definitions

  • You: means the individual or legal entity accessing or using the Scrivs App

  • the App, Scrivs, We, Our, the Scrivs App: means Scrivs

  • Personal Data: means any information that is related to an identified or identifiable individual

  • Device: means any device that can acces the App, such as computer, phone or tablet

Collecting and Using your Personal Data

Scrivs DOES NOT collect any Personal Data or usage data from You or Your Device. The App works offline and stores data locally. The only data stored locally is the alphabets that you add to favorites, in order for them to remain there when You reopen the App.
